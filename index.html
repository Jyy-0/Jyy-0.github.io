Oct. 2020 - June 2021

I mainly develped prediction models based on random forest, neural networks(DNN, LSTM), and studied the data from the Chinese stock market focusing on their daily change. My works are mainly based on Python, and Sklearn and tensorflow. (1) One work I did is make prediction by labeling them into three classes, aiming at getting more stocks be picked will rise than drop in short terms. (2) Another work I did is to further evalute the relationship between my models and the stock market where I found how many stocks be predicted as rise or drop can connect to the whole market's index.(an result example) And that might help build different stratagies in trading.
   From this project, I learned lot of concepts in machine learning, and improved my skills on data analysis and visualization.
