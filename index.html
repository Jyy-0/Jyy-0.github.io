Implement Machine Learning on Financial Data Analysis

Oct. 2020 - June 2021

  This is the project for graduation and my Bachelor’s Degree @ Xi'an Jiaotong University. The goal is to help analyze the stock market and help with further stratagies' formation in trading. I develped prediction models based on random forest and neural networks, and studied the data from the Chinese stock market focusing on their daily change. My works are mainly based on Python, and Sklearn and tensorflow. (1) One work I did is make prediction by labeling them into three classes, aiming at finding more stocks which will rise in short terms with certain precission. (2) Another work I did is to further evalute the relationship between my models and the stock market where I found how many stocks be predicted by my model as rise or drop showed some connections to the whole market's index.(an result example) And that might help build different stratagies in trading.
   From this project, I learned lots of concepts in machine learning, and improved my skills on data analysis and visualization.
